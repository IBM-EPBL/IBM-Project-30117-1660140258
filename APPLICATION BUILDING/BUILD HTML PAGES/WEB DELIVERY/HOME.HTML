FERTILIZER RECOMMENDATION AND DISEASE PREDICTION

INTRO

To assist farmers with technology !!

An automated system is introduced to identify different diseases on plants by checking the symptoms shown on the leaves of the plant. Deep learning techniques are used to identify the diseases and suggest the precautions that can be taken for those diseases. Just upload the pictures of a leaf infested and the AI system of the app will detect the disease. After the disease identification contact the local agricultural expert for help.
